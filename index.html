🎭 Mood System

Slimes have moods that change based on care, events, items, or pure chaos. Each mood affects gameplay and triggers unique roasts!
  • 😊 Happy – Bonus fruits, positive vibes
  • 😢 Sad – Grows slower, roast: "That slime's tears could water a real garden."
  • 😡 Angry – More mutations, roast: "Your slime’s got more rage issues than you."
  • 🤪 Hyper – Random effects, roast: "Either your slime’s on sugar or caffeine."
  • 😌 Chill – Higher rare fruit chance, roast: "This slime’s more relaxed than your weekend plans."
Use mood potions or special items to influence your slimes!
